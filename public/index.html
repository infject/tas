🍻 Jack Jackson: Resonance Duel 🍻

How to Play

Welcome to Jack Jackson: Resonance Duel — a chaotic drinking card game where mages clash through resonance and shots of real-life power.

  • 🎯 Goal: Reduce your opponent’s Stability (❤️) to 0!
  • 🌀 Resonance (Energy): Magical power used to play cards.
  • ❤️ Stability (Health): Lose it all, and you’re out of the duel.
  • 🍺 Drink Potion: Take a real-life sip/shot → gain +1🌀Resonance in-game.
  • ⚡ Overload: Too much drinking or resonance? You might lose control and the game!
  • 👥 Multiplayer: Join or create a room with a code and password to duel your friends online.
  • 🔥 Turn System: Play cards strategically to drain your opponent’s Stability or protect your own.

Remember: This is a drinking game. Drink responsibly. Know your limits. Hydrate. 💧
